Introduction

Welcome to my portfolio. I am a current university student looking to broaden my skill range in modelling and the fundamentals of game design. Here, I have showcased some earlier examples of my work and experience with digital art. Thank you for viewing and have fun exploring the small easter eggs I have planted around the site. For contact feel free to email me at 's5310721@bournemouth.ac.uk' or by visiting my contact page for other social links!


Inspirations

Since I was younger, I've always had an interest in video games. From my first experience with Wii Sports upon release to now catching up with classic Nintendo and Valve titles; video games continue to impact my day to day life through inspiration and general enjoyment. I owe a lot of my nowaday passions to games such as animal crossing for it's iconic and calming music to the character designs in narrative focused games. Since then, I've wanted to tell stories of my own and develop characters audiences are able to both connect with emotionally and project onto the same way I've been doing with some of my favourite titles.
