Introduction

Welcome to my portfolio. I am a current university student looking to broaden my skill range in modelling and the fundamentals of game design. Here, I have showcased some earlier examples of my work and experience with digital art. Thank you for viewing and have fun exploring the small easter eggs I have planted around the site. For contact feel free to email me at 's5310721@bournemouth.ac.uk' or by visiting my contact page for other social links!

Inspirations

Since I was younger, I have taken an interest in game development and how to tell a good story. Most of my most favourite games consist of an indepth and heavy story that has the audience grasping their chairs, and I'm hoping my course will help me develop the skills to be able to do the same. Simiarly, games have given me an interest into character design and world building. My main inspirations come from games such as "Danganronpa", "Shin Megami Tensei", "OneShot" and "Undertale".
