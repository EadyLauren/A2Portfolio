Character Design

I've toyed around with character designs under differnet concepts and inspirations. Creating reference sheets for protagonists or characters in games is one of my favourite aspects of art and I take a big inspiration from video games. Some examples of my inspirations include: "Danganronpa", "Your Turn to Die", "A Hat in Time" "The World ends with You". I also admire the creature designs in the majority of Nintendo series including "Kirby" "Mario, and "Animal Crossing". Developing my art skills is something I aim to work on outside of the cirriculum.
